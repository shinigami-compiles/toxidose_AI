Frequently Asked Questions

Common Doubts
Is this a replacement for my doctor?
No. ToxiDose AI is a research prototype for educational purposes. Always consult qualified healthcare professionals for medical decisions.
How accurate are the predictions?
The model was trained on synthetic data and achieves an R¬≤ score of ~0.85 on test sets. Real-world validation has not been performed.
Is my data stored or shared?
No. All analysis happens in real-time on our server and is not stored. We do not share or sell any data.
What if my medicine isn't in the dropdown?
Our database contains 15 common medicines. If yours isn't listed, the system cannot analyze it. Contact support to request additions.
